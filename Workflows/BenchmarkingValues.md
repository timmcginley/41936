# Benchmarking 
## The list below are the required benchmarking values for Part C

## Target KPIs vs Achieved KPIs
* User
* DGNB
* CO2/User
* Cost

## BEATS
* Total floor area [m2]
* Office area of permanent workspaces [m2]
* Number of permanent workspaces
* Minimum floor-to-ceiling height [m]
* Total Building height [m]

## Environmental Impact
* CO2 per workspace ([(CO2*kg/m2/year)*Total office area (m2)] / Permanent desks) 
* Total Embodied CO2
* CO2 per discipline ( 6 values)
* Estimated reuse percentage [%] (Approximate percentage of reused materials/components included in the proposal, please explain how you came up with the number)

## Cost & Hours
* Total hours
* Hours per discipline ( 6 values)
* Hourly consultancy rate used for each discipline ( 6 values)
* Consultancy cost (Discipline hours * Discipline rate) ( 6 values)
* Estimated Building/Construction cost
  * Arch (e.g. cost of facades, roof, partitions, etc.)
  * Structure (cost of structural systems)
  * Geo (e.g. excavation, foundations, ground improvement work)
  * MEP (cost of MEP systems)
  * Mat (cost of materials and finishes)
